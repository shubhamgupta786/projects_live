TCS

Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) is an Indian multinational information technology (IT) services and consulting company headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India with its largest campus located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. As of February 2021, TCS is the largest IT services company in the world by market capitalisation ($169.2 billion). It is a subsidiary of the Tata Group and operates in 149 locations across 46 countries

Hiring process

Each year TCS hire so many engineers through TCS-NQT (national qualifier test) they hire for mostly Assistant system enginner (or we say TCS Ninja Profile ) and System enginner (or we say it tcs digital profile) salary range for Ninja profile is around 3.5 LPA and for Digital profile is around 7.2LPA

In TCS NQT paper consists of general aptitude, english question and also some 2 coding questions. NQT occurs multiple time in a year and all 3rd and 4th year students are eligible.




Infosys

Infosys Limited is an Indian multinational information technology company that provides business consulting, information technology and outsourcing services. The company was founded in Pune and is headquartered in Bangalore. Infosys is the second-largest Indian IT company after Tata Consultancy Services by 2020 revenue figures and the 602nd largest public company in the world according to Forbes Global 2000 ranking. On 29 July 2021, its market capitalisation was $93.68 billion. The credit rating of the company is CRISIL AAA / Stable / CRISIL A1+ (rating by CRISIL).

Hiring process

Infosys hire through oncampus on selected colleges for system enginner role and selection process consists of test based on aptitude and general coding and compensation for system enginner role is around 3.6LPA another one was Hackwithinfy an all over india coding contest conducted by infosys consists of 3 round and based on performance in round and number of question you solved it offer role like System Enginner specialist with package around 5LPA and power prrogrammer profile wiith package around 8LPA Coding questions are mainly of greedy and Dynamic programmic.



Cognizant

Cognizant is an American multinational technology company that provides business consulting, information technology and outsourcing services. It is headquartered in Teaneck, New Jersey, United States. Cognizant is part of the NASDAQ-100 and trades under CTSH. It was founded as an in-house technology unit of Dun & Bradstreet in 1994, and started serving external clients in 1996.
